Investment Pools

Explore the benefits and features of our precious metal investment pools.

How the Pools Work

Members' funds are used to buy units in pools they select. These pools are primarily invested in precious metals (95% or more), including Krugerrands (1Oz, 1/10Oz Gold, and 1Oz Silver). These assets are globally recognized and highly liquid. Note: Gold Krugerrands do not incur VAT.

Valuation & Flexibility

Pools are valued daily. Members may buy or redeem units at the current unit price and can customize deposit allocation across available pools.

Ownership & Control

While the Co-op manages the pools, members own them via units. Units can be redeemed for cash or metals, and members can transfer or switch between pools with appropriate notice.

Membership Requirement

Only members of Bullion Beperk Co-operative Limited can invest. Personal data and holdings are confidential. Members access personalized data and unit values via secure login.

Member Options After Investing

Members may withdraw units for cash and/or metals with notice. Physical collection is available in Pretoria or Johannesburg, or delivery via secure courier.
